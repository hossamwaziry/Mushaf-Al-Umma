Privacy Policy for Quran App

This privacy policy applies to our Quran reading application that collects no user data

Last Updated: August 11, 2025

✓ No Data Collection

Our Quran application is designed with privacy as a core principle. We want to assure you that:

  • The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or usage data
  • We do not access your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • We do not track which pages you visit or how much time you spend in the app
  • We do not gather any information about your device or operating system
  • We do not request or store any personally identifiable information

✓ Local Data Storage

The application functions entirely offline after initial setup:

  • The Quran text is downloaded to your device's local Realm database during the first launch
  • All data including bookmarks, reading progress, and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • This locally stored data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application

✓ No Third-Party Services

To ensure maximum privacy protection:

  • The application does not integrate with any analytics services
  • There are no advertising networks or tracking libraries included
  • We do not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any
  • The application does not require internet access after the initial download of Quran text

✓ Children's Privacy

Our application is safe for users of all ages:

  • We do not knowingly interact with or collect data from children under 13
  • The application contains no social features or communication capabilities
  • Parents can feel safe allowing children to use this application as it collects no data and has no external interactions

✓ Security

While we don't collect any data, we still prioritize security:

  • All Quran text is downloaded via secure HTTPS connection
  • Local data is stored in an encrypted Realm database for protection
  • The application requests no unnecessary permissions on your device

✓ Policy Changes

We are committed to maintaining a no-data-collection policy:

  • Any changes to this privacy policy will be clearly communicated within the application
  • We will never introduce data collection without explicit user consent
  • This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect any changes in regulations
