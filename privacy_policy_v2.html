Privacy Policy for Mushaf Al-Umma

Last Updated: August 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Mushaf Al-Umma app (the "Application"), a free mobile application created by Hossam Waziry (the "Service Provider").

No Data Collection

The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or usage data from users. This includes:

  • No IP addresses, device identifiers, or location data
  • No usage statistics (e.g., time spent, pages visited)
  • No personal information (e.g., name, email, contacts)

Since the app is a simple Quran reader with no accounts, analytics, or ads, no user data is processed or shared with any third parties.

No Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate any third-party services (e.g., analytics, advertising, or tracking tools) that could access user data.

No Data Retention

Since no data is collected, there is no data to retain or delete.

Children's Privacy

The Application is safe for all ages and does not knowingly interact with or collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
