Data Collection Policy

Mushaf Al-Umma is designed with a fundamental commitment to user privacy. We adhere to a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data
  • We do not access your device's identifiers, location, or network information
  • No analytics or tracking mechanisms are implemented within the application
  • User interactions with the Quran text are entirely private and local to your device

Local Data Storage

Mushaf Al-Umma operates as a self-contained application with all data stored locally:

  • Quran text is downloaded to your device's secure Realm database during initial setup
  • All personal usage data (bookmarks, reading progress, notes) is stored exclusively on your device
  • This locally stored data never leaves your device and is inaccessible to external parties
  • Data remains available offline and can be permanently deleted through standard application uninstallation

Security & Privacy Safeguards

We implement multiple layers of protection for your Quran reading experience:

  • Quran text is downloaded via encrypted HTTPS connection from trusted sources
  • Local database employs industry-standard encryption for sensitive user annotations
  • The application requests no unnecessary permissions on your device
  • Regular security audits ensure the application maintains its privacy-first design

Children's Privacy

Mushaf Al-Umma is suitable for users of all ages:

  • We do not knowingly interact with or collect data from children under 13
  • The application contains no communication features or external interactions
  • Parents can confidently allow children to use this application knowing their privacy is protected

Third-Party Services

To maintain absolute privacy, Mushaf Al-Umma contains no external integrations:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.) are integrated
  • No advertising networks are included in the application
  • The application does not require internet access after initial Quran text download
  • We maintain no data-sharing relationships with third parties

Policy Updates

Our commitment to privacy remains unwavering:

  • Any changes to this policy will be clearly communicated within the application
  • We will never introduce data collection without explicit user consent
  • This privacy policy may be updated to comply with regulatory requirements
